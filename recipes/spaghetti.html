How to make Spaghetti

Description

A classic, comforting spaghetti dish with a rich tomato and garlic sauce, perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a cozy meal with family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Toss the cooked spaghetti in the sauce until well coated.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese.
